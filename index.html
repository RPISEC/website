Active CTF Competitor

RPISEC plays both competitively and casually in a number of computer security CTFs throughout the school year, typically placing among the top teams in the world.

Weekly Meetings

We host meetings and events on a weekly basis led by fellow students to teach a wide variety of practical computer security skills that translate directly to the real world.

Educational Resources

We work to obtain educational resources necessary for our members to explore computer security and help connect them with both internships and full time job opportunities.
